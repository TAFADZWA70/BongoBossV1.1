Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

To book a service: 1) Browse available services, 2) Select a service provider, 3) Choose a package, 4) Select date and time, 5) Confirm booking details, 6) Complete payment. You'll receive a confirmation email once your booking is confirmed.

How can I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Go to "My Bookings", select the booking you want to modify, and click the cancel or reschedule button. Please note our cancellation policy: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the service. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and mobile payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

How do I leave a review?

After your service is completed, go to "My Bookings", find the completed booking, and click "Leave Review". You can rate the service and provider, and share your experience to help other customers.

What if I'm not satisfied with a service?

If you're not satisfied, please contact us within 48 hours of service completion through the contact form below. Provide your booking details and a description of the issue. We'll work with you and the provider to resolve the matter.

How do I add providers to my favorites?

When browsing services or viewing a provider's profile, click the heart icon to add them to your favorites. You can view all your favorite providers in the "Favorites" section.

Safety & Security Tips

Secure Payments

Always use the platform's secure payment system. Never pay outside the platform or share your banking details directly with providers.

Verify Providers

Check provider ratings, reviews, and verification badges before booking. Read feedback from previous customers.

Use Platform Messages

Keep all communication within the BongoBoss messaging system for your protection and to maintain a record of conversations.

Read Service Details

Carefully review service descriptions, pricing, and terms before booking. Contact the provider if you have questions.
