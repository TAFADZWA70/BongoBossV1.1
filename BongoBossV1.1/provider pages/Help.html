Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create and manage my service packages?

Go to "My Services" in your dashboard. Click "Add New Service" to create a package. Include detailed descriptions, pricing, duration, and high-quality images. You can edit or deactivate packages at any time. Make your packages clear and comprehensive to attract more bookings.

How do I accept or decline booking requests?

When you receive a booking request, you'll get a notification. Go to "Bookings" and review the request details. Accept if you're available, or decline with a polite reason. Try to respond within 24 hours to maintain good customer relations and platform ratings.

When and how do I receive payments?

Payments are released after service completion and customer confirmation. Payouts are processed weekly to your registered bank account. You can track your earnings in the "Earnings" section. Ensure your banking details are up to date in your profile settings.

How can I improve my profile and attract more customers?

Complete your profile with professional photos, detailed service descriptions, and competitive pricing. Respond quickly to inquiries, maintain high ratings by providing excellent service, and encourage satisfied customers to leave reviews. Keep your availability calendar updated.

What should I do if a customer cancels a booking?

If a customer cancels more than 24 hours before the service, you won't receive payment. For cancellations within 24 hours, you may receive partial compensation according to our cancellation policy. The booking status will automatically update in your dashboard.

How do I handle disputes or issues with customers?

First, try to resolve the issue directly with the customer through professional communication. If unresolved, contact our support team through the form below within 48 hours. Provide your booking ID and a detailed explanation. We'll mediate to find a fair solution.

Can I set my own schedule and availability?

Yes! You have full control over your schedule. Update your availability calendar regularly to show when you can accept bookings. Block out dates when you're unavailable. Customers can only book during your available time slots.

What are the platform fees and commission rates?

BongoBoss charges a service fee on each completed booking, which is deducted before payout. The exact percentage is detailed in your provider agreement. You can view the breakdown of each transaction in your "Earnings" dashboard. All fees are transparent with no hidden charges.

Best Practices for Success

Maintain Quality Service

Consistently deliver excellent service to earn positive reviews and repeat customers. Quality service leads to higher ratings and more bookings on the platform.

Respond Promptly

Reply to booking requests and customer messages within 24 hours. Quick response times improve your visibility in search results and customer satisfaction.

Use Quality Photos

Upload clear, professional photos of your work and services. High-quality images significantly increase booking rates and help customers make informed decisions.

Professional Communication

Keep all communication professional and courteous. Use the platform messaging system for all customer interactions to maintain records and protection.

Update Your Calendar

Keep your availability calendar current to avoid double bookings and cancellations. Regular updates help maintain a professional reputation.

Competitive Pricing

Research similar services and price competitively while reflecting your quality and experience. Clear, transparent pricing builds customer trust.
